This is how to make pancakes


(these are them)


Description

Ahh pancakes. The fluffiest of the flat food family.The millenary go-to breakfast for the eight year old inside.

Today I'm gonna teach you how to up your pancake game. (Yes, birthday candles and rainbow sprinkles are an essential part of this recipe)


Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Butter (melted)
  • 1 egg
  • Vanilla extract
  • Rainbow sprinkles and bday candles

Steps

  1. Walk the walk
  2. Talk the talk
  3. Fake till you make it
  4. Flip when you start seeing bubbles around the edges
  5. Sprinkle the sprinkles
