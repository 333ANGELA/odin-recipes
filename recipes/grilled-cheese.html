This is how to make grilled cheese


look at it!


Description

Melted cheese between two slices of bread. Why is it so good? I'm gonna show you how to make it even better.


Ingredients

  • Bread (your favorite kind)
  • Cheese (your favorite kind)
  • Butter

Extras

  • A second kind of cheese (of your preference)
  • Salt
  • Tomatoes
  • Pesto

Steps

  1. Warm the pan
  2. Spread butter on a slice of bread and place it on the pan face down
  3. Put all your ingredients on top
  4. Place second piece of bread on top
  5. Flip when you start seeing the cheese melting
  6. Voila!
